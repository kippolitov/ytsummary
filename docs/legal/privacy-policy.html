Privacy Policy — Video Knowledge Panel

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Video Knowledge Panel ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that summarizes YouTube videos and lets you chat about their content. This page explains what data the extension handles, how it's used, and what choices you have.

What the extension accesses

The extension only reads the page you're actively viewing, and only after you click its toolbar icon (via Chrome's activeTab permission) — it never reads pages in the background and never requests broad access to your browsing.

When you open the panel on a YouTube video, the extension reads:

  • The video's transcript/captions, title, and channel name
  • Any follow-up questions or messages you type into the Chat tab

How that data is used

This content is sent to a backend service (an Azure Function that we operate) which calls Azure OpenAI to generate a summary, key topics, step-by-step breakdown, references, or a chat response. The content is processed to produce that response and is not used for any other purpose — not advertising, not profiling, not resale.

What is stored, and where

  • In your browser: the current video's summary and chat history are kept in chrome.storage.session, which Chrome automatically clears when you close the browser. Your theme preference (light/dark/system) is kept in localStorage until you change it or clear site data.
  • On our servers: nothing is persisted after your request completes. Our backend is a stateless API with no database — video transcripts and chat messages are processed in memory to generate a response and then discarded. Standard operational telemetry (request counts, latency, errors) is collected via Azure Application Insights to keep the service running, but this does not include the content of your video transcripts or chat messages.

Third parties

The only third-party processor involved is Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service, which processes the content described above to generate responses, under Microsoft's own enterprise data handling commitments. We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or any service that profiles or resells your data. We do not sell your data to anyone, under any circumstances.

Permissions the extension requests

  • activeTab / scripting — to read the transcript of the video you're actively viewing, only when you click the toolbar icon
  • storage — to cache the current video's summary and chat history locally, and remember your theme preference
  • sidePanel — to display the extension's UI in Chrome's side panel

Your choices

Since nothing is stored server-side and browser-side data is session-scoped or clears with your theme setting, there is little persistent data to manage. You can remove all locally cached data at any time by closing your browser, clearing the extension's site data in Chrome settings, or uninstalling the extension.

Children's privacy

The extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
